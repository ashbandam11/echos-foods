Classic Traditional Filipino Dish

Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is made by chicken legs in a sauce made up of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and black pepper.

Pork Sisig

Pork sisig is a popular Filipino dish. It is composed of minced pork, chopped onion, and chicken liver.

Nilagang Baka

This Filipino-style boiled soup with fork-tender beef shanks, tendon, and vegetables is hearty and tasty.

Beef Kaldereta

Beef Caldereta is simmered to tenderness in a spicy tomato sauce. Chockfull of potatoes, bell peppers, and green olives.

Filipino Sweet Frozen Desserts


Halo-halo

Halo-halo is a Filipino-style shaved ice made with sweetened beans, fruits, and jellies and topped with milk, Leche flan, purple yam jam, and ice cream.

Mais Con Yelo

Mais con yelo is a layered dessert consisting of sweet corn, shaved or crushed ice, sweet milk, and a scoop of ice cream. Its perfect for kids!

Sago Gulaman

Sago at Gulaman with sago pearls, grass jelly, and brown sugar syrup is a tropical cooler you’ll love year-round. It’s refreshing and delicious as it is fun to eat!

Iskrambol

This Ice Scramble or Iskrambol is a fun and easy-to-make Filipino dessert made of shaved ice, sugar, condensed milk, and pineapple syrup.
